Meet Paisley

Where Passion
Meets Precision

At Paisley Dior Hair, every appointment is more than just a service — it’s a personalized experience. I take the time to understand your hair goals, lifestyle, and vision so that every cut, color, and style feels authentically you.

From classic cuts and lived-in color to full transformations and special-occasion styling, I’m here to help you leave the chair feeling confident and beautiful.

What I Offer

Services

From the perfect cut to a full color transformation, every service is customized to your hair type, texture, and personal style.

Lived-In Blonding

Custom foil and blonding services designed for a natural, sun-kissed look that grows out beautifully. Starting at $200.

Color Services

From toner refreshes to full grey coverage and all-over color, we’ll find your perfect shade. Starting at $135.

Cuts & Styling

Precision cuts, maintenance trims, and bang shaping tailored to your hair type and lifestyle. Starting at $25.

Extensions

Seamless, natural-looking extensions customized to add length, volume, or both. Consultation required.

Special Occasion Styling

Wash and styles, updos, and event-ready looks for your most important moments. Starting at $65.
