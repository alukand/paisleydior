Meet Paisley

Where Passion
Meets Precision

At Paisley Dior Hair, every appointment is more than just a service — it’s a personalized experience. I take the time to understand your hair goals, lifestyle, and vision so that every cut, color, and style feels authentically you.

From classic cuts and lived-in color to full transformations and special-occasion styling, I’m here to help you leave the chair feeling confident and beautiful.

Explore Services

What I Offer

Services

From the perfect cut to a full color transformation, every service is customized to your hair type, texture, and personal style.

Haircuts & Trims

Precision cuts tailored to your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle — from fresh trims to bold new looks.

Color & Highlights

Full color, partial highlights, lowlights, and color corrections — expertly formulated for rich, dimensional results.

Balayage & Ombré

Hand-painted, sun-kissed color that blends seamlessly for a natural, lived-in look with beautiful grow-out.

Blowouts & Styling

Smooth, voluminous blowouts and polished styling for everyday glam or a special night out.

Extensions

Seamless, natural-looking hair extensions for added length, volume, or both — customized to match your hair perfectly.

Updos & Bridal

Elegant updos, braids, and event-ready styles for weddings, proms, and every special occasion in between.

Deep Conditioning & Treatments

Restorative deep conditioning, keratin smoothing, and bond-building treatments to strengthen and revitalize your hair.

Toning & Gloss

Custom toners and gloss treatments to refresh your color, neutralize brassiness, and add brilliant shine.

Consultations

A one-on-one consultation to discuss your hair goals, assess your hair health, and create a personalized plan.
